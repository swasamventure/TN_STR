Good to know

Frequently asked questions.

How many guests can the cabin accommodate?

ArborVista Retreat is designed for up to six guests. Please confirm sleeping arrangements and current occupancy rules on the Airbnb listing before booking.

Where is the cabin located?

The retreat is in Sevierville, Tennessee, with convenient access to Pigeon Forge, Gatlinburg, Dollywood and Great Smoky Mountains National Park. The exact address is shared with confirmed guests.

How do I check pricing and availability?

Use the “Book on Airbnb” buttons throughout the website for live pricing, minimum-stay requirements and available dates.

Is there a private hot tub?

Yes. The retreat includes a private hot tub for registered guests. Please follow all posted safety rules during your stay.

Is the cabin suitable for children?

The property is family friendly, but children should be supervised at all times, especially around stairs, decks, the hot tub and fire pit.
