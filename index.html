Welcome home

A cabin stay with room to reconnect.

ArborVista Retreat is a quiet, private retreat inside gated Shagbark Resort. Two main-level suites give adults privacy, while the open loft—with two twin beds and arcade games—creates a fun space for kids and teens.

Spend the day exploring Pigeon Forge, Dollywood, Gatlinburg, or the national park, then return to a peaceful wooded setting, three fireplaces, an expansive deck, and your own private hot tub.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How many guests can the cabin accommodate, and what is the bed and bath layout?+

The cabin comfortably sleeps six across two main-level suites: one King and one Queen, each with an ensuite bathroom. The open upper loft has two twin beds and arcade games, making it ideal for kids or teens. The cabin has 2.5 bathrooms: two full ensuite bathrooms plus one main-level half bath.

What time is check-in and check-out?+

Check-in begins at 4:00 PM and checkout is by 10:00 AM.

How does check-in work?+

A personalized smart-lock door code is sent three days before arrival. No office stop or key pickup is required.

Is parking available?+

Yes. Free off-street parking is available on-site for approximately 2–3 vehicles.

Are pets allowed?+

No. ArborVista Retreat is a strictly pet-free home. A $500 fee plus immediate removal without refund may apply to violations.

Is smoking or vaping allowed?+

No smoking or vaping is permitted inside the cabin. A $2,000 cleaning and deodorizing fee may apply to violations.

Is there an age requirement to book?+

Yes. The primary guest must be at least 23 years old to reserve.

Is Wi-Fi available?+

Yes. Strong high-speed Wi-Fi is included throughout the cabin for streaming and remote work.

What resort amenities are included?+

Guests receive complimentary access to Shagbark Resort amenities, including the seasonal pool (Memorial Day through Labor Day, 9 AM–9 PM), pickleball, tennis, basketball, playground, frisbee golf, cornhole, fitness trail, dog park, picnic pavilion, and gated entrance. Availability and rules are controlled by the resort.

How far is the cabin from Pigeon Forge, Gatlinburg, and Dollywood?+

The Island in Pigeon Forge is about 9 miles away, Dollywood is 12 miles, Great Smoky Mountains National Park is 14 miles, and central Gatlinburg attractions such as Anakeesta and the SkyBridge are about 17 miles away. Drive times vary with traffic and season.

What is the cancellation policy?+

The cancellation terms displayed during booking apply. For Airbnb reservations, review the current Airbnb cancellation policy at checkout. A processing charge of 3% or $75, whichever is higher, may apply where permitted by the booking terms.

Is a rental agreement required?+

Yes. A signed electronic rental agreement is required after booking and will be sent shortly after the reservation is confirmed.

Where do I see current pricing and availability?+

Live nightly rates, fees, minimum stays, and open dates are always shown on the Airbnb listing. Pricing is not duplicated here so it does not become outdated.
